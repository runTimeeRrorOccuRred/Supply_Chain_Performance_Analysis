# 📊 Supply Chain Performance Analysis – AtliQ Mart

## 🧩 Problem Statement

AtliQ Mart is facing customer churn due to poor delivery service levels. The objective of this project is to analyze On-Time (OT), In-Full (IF), and OTIF performance to identify service gaps, uncover root causes, and improve overall supply chain efficiency before expanding to new cities.

---

## 📌 Key Insights

### 🔴 Overall Performance

* OTIF is critically low at **29% vs 66% target**, indicating severe service failure.
* OT% (~59%) and IF% (~53%) are both significantly below targets, showing issues in both **delivery timing and order fulfillment**.

---

### 🌍 City-Level Insights

* All cities (Surat, Ahmedabad, Vadodara) are underperforming → indicates a **system-wide issue**.
* **Vadodara is the worst-performing city**, with the lowest OT, IF, and OTIF.

---

### 🚚 Delivery Performance Insights

* Only ~48% of orders are delivered on time.
* ~41% orders are delayed → major logistics inefficiency.
* Fast deliveries are very low (~10%) → lack of operational efficiency.

---

### 📦 OTIF Failure Analysis

* **Incomplete deliveries (~42%) are the primary cause of OTIF failure**.
* Late deliveries (~33%) are the second major issue.
* ~24% failures are due to both late and incomplete deliveries.

---

### 🏬 Customer-Level Insights

* **Coolblue, Lotus Mart, Acclaimed Stores** → lowest On-Time performance.
* **Elite Mart, Sorefoz Mart, Vijay Stores** → lowest In-Full performance.

---

### 📍 City + Customer Root Cause

* Ahmedabad → Sorefoz Mart & Lotus Mart have very low IF (~17–24%)
* Surat → Info Stores & Acclaimed Stores underperform in IF (~19–22%)
* Vadodara → Elite Mart, Vijay Stores & Coolblue show lowest IF (~16–22%)

👉 Indicates **city-specific inventory and fulfillment issues**

---

### 🛒 Category Insight

* Dairy contributes ~59% of total orders → highest demand category.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

### 🔧 Operational Improvements

* Improve **inventory planning** to reduce incomplete deliveries.
* Optimize **delivery routes and scheduling** to reduce delays.
* Strengthen **warehouse and dispatch efficiency**.

---

### 📍 Focus Areas

* Immediate operational improvement required in **Vadodara**.
* Fix **inventory allocation issues in Ahmedabad & Surat** for key customers.

---

### 📊 Strategic Actions

* Implement **real-time tracking systems** for delivery monitoring.
* Align teams around **OTIF as the primary KPI**.
* Improve **demand forecasting** to avoid stockouts.

---

## 🚀 Project Outcome

This dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly identify underperforming customers, regions, and root causes, allowing faster decision-making and improved service levels.
